Awakening

First, confirm NFC is on.
Open Settings, search for NFC, and switch it on if it is not already.

Then find where it wakes.
On iPhone, that place lives along the top center edge.
On Android, it should be in the upper half of the back —
usually near the camera.

Hold the charm there. Move slowly.
When you find the right place, your phone will know.

One thing to be aware of: a magnetic phone case — the kind that lets your phone cling to a car mount without a bracket — will silence the charm. The magnets interfere with the NFC reader and prevent it from waking. Simply remove the case, and the magic returns.

A single tap is all it takes.
No app to install.
No pairing.
Just tap, and the journey starts.

Binding

A guide will open. You will be asked to shape the memory:

  • Create a Keeper account
  • Accept the Memory Charm license
  • Choose a memory — video, photo, or audio
  • Give it a title and a short description
  • Set how it may be shared
  • Seal the charm

For the first fourteen days,
the memory stays soft — editable, still taking shape.

After that, it settles into its final form —
a story, ready to be told.
